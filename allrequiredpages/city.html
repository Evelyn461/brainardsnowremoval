Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will crews arrive after a snowfall?

Response times depend on contract tier and storm intensity; seasonal priority clients receive the fastest deployment. We publish expected windows in each seasonal agreement.

Do you remove snow from private property only?

We clear private driveways, walkways, and commercial lots under signed agreements; we can coordinate with municipal crews when services overlap.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes.Brainard Snow Removal maintains insurance and operator certifications appropriate for commercial and residential contracts.

Can you handle roof snow and ice dams?

Yes, roof work is performed by certified crews using fall-protection and load-handling procedures.

How are salt and de-icers applied?

We use calibrated spreaders and document materials and amounts per visit for transparency.

How do I sign up for a seasonal plan?

Contact our office to choose a tier; we’ll outline triggers, response windows, and contract terms.
